## CTA Section Specification

**File:** `src/app/page.tsx`

**Placement:** Between the hero section and the `#how` section (after the closing `</section>` of the hero, before `<section id="how"`). This is the highest-visibility position ‚Äî users who have entered their address and seen the hero will encounter the CTA before scrolling to the explainer.

**Link target:** `/preview` (internal Next.js route ‚Äî use `<Link href="/preview">` from `next/link`, no `target="_blank"`)

**Rationale for `/preview`:** The user decision (resolved during research) is that the CTA links to `/preview`, the AI tool page within this same app. Do not link to `https://roofing.sydney` home ‚Äî that is self-referential and creates a no-op.

**Section structure:**

```tsx
import Link from "next/link";

// Insert between hero </section> and <section id="how">
<section className="border-t border-border">
  <div className="mx-auto max-w-5xl px-6 py-12 text-center">
    <h2 className="text-xl font-semibold tracking-tight text-foreground">
      Ready to see your new roof?
    </h2>
    <p className="mt-3 max-w-md mx-auto text-sm text-muted">
      Use our free AI visualiser to preview Colorbond colours on your actual
      home ‚Äî then book a no-obligation quote.
    </p>
    <Link
      href="/preview"
      className="mt-6 inline-flex items-center rounded-full bg-foreground px-5 py-2.5 text-sm font-semibold text-background hover:opacity-90"
    >
      Check My Roof Design
    </Link>
  </div>
</section>
```

**Dimensions:**
- Section: `border-t border-border` top divider, `py-12` vertical (48px)
- Container: `max-w-5xl px-6` ‚Äî matches all other sections
- Heading: `text-xl font-semibold tracking-tight` ‚Äî matches Step heading hierarchy
- Subtext: `text-sm text-muted max-w-md mx-auto` ‚Äî constrained width for readability
- Button: `rounded-full bg-foreground px-5 py-2.5 text-sm font-semibold text-background hover:opacity-90`
  - Pill shape matches nav "Free quote" button
  - Dark fill (`bg-foreground`) gives higher visual weight than nav button (which uses `bg-background border`)
  - No icon ‚Äî copy is self-explanatory

**Background:** `bg-background` (white, default) ‚Äî same as hero. The section below (`#how`) already has `bg-surface` to create the alternating rhythm, so this CTA section reads as part of the hero band.
